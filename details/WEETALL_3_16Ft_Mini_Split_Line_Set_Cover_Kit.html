WEETALL 3" 16Ft Mini Split Line Set Cover Kit

Upgrade the look of your HVAC system with the WEETALL 3" 16Ft mini split line set cover kit. This stylish solution is perfect for homeowners and installers who want to blend their equipment seamlessly into the home's exterior. The kit includes 10 straight ducts and 1 flexible duct, designed to fit your line set precisely and cover imperfections without the need for additional parts.

Key Features

  • Perfectly sized 4" x 16ft line set cover with 10 straight ducts and 1 flexible duct
  • Paintable design that allows you to match your home’s exterior color for a clean, modern look
  • Durable upgraded Polypropylene (PP) material that resists weather and extreme temperatures
  • Easy DIY installation with all necessary components included – no tools required!
  • Ideal for both indoor and outdoor use on various mini split and central AC systems

Why You'll Love It

This line set cover doesn't just protect your system – it enhances its appearance too. The polypropylene construction ensures it remains flexible and strong even in harsh weather. Plus, its customizable finish lets you blend the unit into your property, giving it a sleek and integrated look that complements your home's design. Whether you're replacing old covers or upgrading your system, the WEETALL kit offers hassle-free installation and long-lasting performance you can rely on.

The flexible design of the WEETALL 3" line set cover makes it suitable for a wide range of installations, from indoor mini split systems to outdoor central air conditioners and heat pumps.
