Upgrade Your Cooling Experience with the tBesme Sliding Door Air Conditioner Kit

This versatile sliding door AC vent kit is designed to seamlessly integrate with your portable AC unit, providing a secure and reliable seal. Perfect for those who need efficient cooling without compromising on comfort, it's ideal for sliding doors and windows of various sizes and orientations.

The kit features a 5.9 inch diameter coupler that works with both clockwise and anti-clockwise installation, offering flexibility for different setups. Its adjustable plates can be extended up to 95 inches, allowing you to customize the fit for your specific door or window height. This ensures a perfect match and optimal performance of your AC unit.

Crafted from 2mm thick reinforced PVC, this vent kit is built to last, offering stability and durability in any environment. The included 6 x 17.3 inch plates, 2 x 3m sealing tape, and screws provide everything needed for a secure, leak-free installation, helping you achieve maximum cooling efficiency.

Whether you're a homeowner or a renter, this portable AC sliding door adapter simplifies the installation process. Its user-friendly design and compatibility with sliding doors make it an excellent addition to any cooling system. Keep your living space cool and comfortable, no matter the season.

With the tBesme sliding door AC vent kit, you can enjoy uninterrupted cooling and energy savings. The inclusion of sealing tape and strong fastening options ensures your AC unit stays firmly in place, delivering consistent performance and reducing unnecessary energy waste.
