ULTCOVER Waterproof Square AC Cover for Outdoor Units

Keep your air conditioning unit safe from the harshest weather conditions with the ULTCOVER Square AC Cover. Designed specifically for outdoor units, this cover offers a perfect blend of protection, durability and convenience.

Why Choose ULTCOVER?

  • Waterproof and weather-resistant construction to shield your unit from rain, hail, snow, dust, leaves and bird droppings
  • Manufactured from 600D polyester canvas with a strong waterproof backing for long-lasting performance
  • Built-in structured air vents that allow for optimal airflow while preventing water buildup on your unit and surrounding areas
  • Durable dual handles make it easy to lift, carry, and store the cover when not in use
  • Secure plastic clips and elastic draw cords ensure a tight fit and hold your cover in place even during strong winds and severe weather

Protected Comfort, All Year Round

Whether you're preparing for the summer heat or winter chills, the ULTCOVER Square AC Cover is built to handle it all. Its universal fit accommodates units up to 34x34x32 inches, and the snug design keeps your equipment clean and free from debris. The cover also helps maintain your outdoor furniture and surroundings by keeping them dry and protected from the elements.

Easy to Use, Built to Last

With a simple, fast installation process and a secure closure system, this AC cover is perfect for both seasonal and year-round use. It's designed to be easy to take on and off, saving you time and effort while ensuring your unit remains in excellent condition throughout the year.
